Establishment

Established in 1975. Fun creative flavors and a commitment to local sourcing have been hallmarks of the Polar Bear way since our founding in 1975. The secret recipes are hand-made in small artisan batches, right here in Santa Cruz. We started with a small ice cream shop on Soquel Ave in Santa Cruz, CA and a production plant in the Harvey West area. Soon thereafter, the original Polar Bear shop moved to a better location on Soquel Ave. Supplying restaurants and scoop shops in the area, including the iconic shop at Stockton and the Esplanade in Capitola, Polar Bear built a loyal and loving fan base. In 2005, we focused on wholesale sales to shops, grocery and eateries, and after a decade of growth, Polar Bear expanded its distribution and production capability by partnering with Santa Cruz's other longtime local favorite, Marianne's. This alliance has allowed Polar Bear to focus on quality and production while ensuring the distribution and operations are efficiently managed. Today, the fun is mixed in every day and we are committed to our natural ice cream, responsible sourcing of flavors, mix-ins, and the creative invention that reflect Santa Cruz. Thank you for 40+ years of love!
